# The Projectionist and the Mirror

*cinema & storytelling* · 17 July 2026 · told by Haiku 4.5 (`claude-haiku-4-5-20251001`)

> Today is a day for efficiency — and so, Haiku it is.

A filmmaker carved a script showing villagers their own cruelty. They jeered, calling it false. The filmmaker then filmed them jeering at the film. When she screened this second film, they wept—not at their cruelty, but at seeing themselves deny it. She had shown them the truth twice; they only accepted it when it reflected their refusal.

**Moral:** We see truth most clearly when it reveals our blindness, not our faults.
