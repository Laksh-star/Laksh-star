# The Captain and the Crossroads

*leadership & management* · 20 July 2026 · told by Haiku 4.5 (`claude-haiku-4-5-20251001`)

> Today is a day for efficiency — and so, Haiku it is.

A ship's captain, unable to choose between two routes, ordered the crew to sail straight ahead. The crew, confused, drifted sideways into shallows. When rocks tore the hull, the captain cried, "Why did no one decide?" His first mate replied, "We waited for you to choose, Captain. Indecision chose for us."

**Moral:** A bad decision navigates; indecision shipwrecks the entire voyage.
