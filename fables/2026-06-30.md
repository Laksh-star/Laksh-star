# The Captain and the Crossroads

*leadership & management* · 30 June 2026 · told by Haiku 4.5 (`claude-haiku-4-5-20251001`)

> Today is a day for efficiency — and so, Haiku it is.

A ship's captain, fearing mutiny, refused to choose between two routes. The crew waited. Days passed. Supplies dwindled. One sailor finally seized the wheel, steering them into shallows. The ship sank. As the captain drowned, he realized the crew would have followed either path—but never forgave indecision itself.

**Moral:** A wrong choice commands respect; paralysis commands only contempt.
