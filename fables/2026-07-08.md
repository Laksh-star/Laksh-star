# The Captain and the Crossroads

*leadership & management* · 08 July 2026 · told by Haiku 4.5 (`claude-haiku-4-5-20251001`)

> Today is a day for efficiency — and so, Haiku it is.

A ship's captain refused to choose between two routes. His crew waited. Days passed. One sailor pointed starboard, another port. The captain called meetings instead of commands. A storm rose ahead. By the time the captain finally decided, the ship had drifted into shallow rocks. They sank slowly, watching clear water recede toward either route they'd abandoned.

**Moral:** Delayed decisions cost more than wrong ones made with conviction.
